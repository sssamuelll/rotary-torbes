About Us

ROTARY TORBES

During these 38 years of operation, our club has carried out social work and community service consisting of medical care days, cash donations and supplies to various institutions, as well as fundraising events that have contributed significant sums of money, for the purchase of equipment and construction works in our Medical Attention centers.

Through our Torbes Rotary Medical-Surgical Unit Foundation umqrt, a fundamental pillar of community service, a solidary and high-quality health service has been provided, which has been growing gradually in a wide range of medical specialties. At our Propatria headquarters, there are two consulting rooms and Laboratory areas, which provide care to low-income communities, with optimal levels of efficiency. Similarly, in our surgery center at the Barrio la Victoria headquarters, two operating rooms are in full operation, which offer hospitalization and surgery in low-risk specialties and minimal care. These centers have been the flags of our action towards the communities, and represent a good part of our mission over time, understanding that there is work to be done that will allow us, in the near future, to reach levels of care typical of a clinic private.
